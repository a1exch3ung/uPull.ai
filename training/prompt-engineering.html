The Prompt Engineering Pathway

Six progressive stages — follow them in order or jump to the stage that matches your current level.

1

Stage 1 — Foundations (Beginner · Free)

2–3 CPD credits · No prior experience needed

Understand what large language models are, why the wording of a prompt changes the output, and how to write your first clear instruction. All resources are free and require no technical background.

▶ Prompt Eng. Intro – Andrej Karpathy (YouTube) Learn Prompting – Free Guide
2

Stage 2 — Core Techniques (Beginner · Free)

3–5 CPD credits · After Stage 1

Learn the fundamental techniques: zero-shot, one-shot, few-shot, role prompting, and instruction formatting. Practice rewriting vague requests into precise, effective prompts.

▶ Few-Shot & Zero-Shot – DeepLearning.AI (YouTube) DAIR.AI Prompting Guide
3

Stage 3 — Intermediate Strategies (Intermediate · Free)

4–6 CPD credits · After Stage 2

Explore chain-of-thought reasoning, step-by-step decomposition, and structured output formatting (JSON, tables, bullet lists). Apply these to NHS document drafting and summarisation tasks.

DeepLearning.AI – ChatGPT Prompt Eng. (Free) ▶ Chain-of-Thought Prompting (YouTube)
4

Stage 4 — Advanced Techniques (Advanced · Free)

6–8 CPD credits · After Stage 3

Master tree-of-thought, self-consistency, ReAct prompting, and meta-prompting. Learn how to write system prompts and personas for NHS-facing AI tools and copilots.

Anthropic Academy – Prompt Engineering (Free) ▶ Advanced Prompting Techniques (YouTube)
5

Stage 5 — Prompt Systems & APIs (Advanced · Free)

8–12 CPD credits · Technical track — developers & digital leads

Design reusable prompt templates, system message architectures, and prompt chaining pipelines. Understand RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation) and how to build safe, auditable prompt systems for NHS deployments.

DeepLearning.AI – Building Systems (Free) ▶ RAG Explained (YouTube)
6

Stage 6 — NHS Practical Capstone (Advanced · Free)

5 bonus CPD credits · Apply everything in your own organisation

Write and test a real prompt library for one NHS workflow of your choice — e.g. patient letter drafting, ward handover summaries, or policy Q&A. Submit to uPull.ai for a shareable CPD record and case study consideration.
